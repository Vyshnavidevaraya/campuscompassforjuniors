Student Clubs & Activities

College life extends far beyond classrooms. Student clubs provide opportunities to develop technical, creative, leadership, and communication skills while building lifelong friendships and unforgettable memories.

Why Join a Club?

Joining clubs is one of the best ways to enhance your college experience. Whether you're interested in coding, music, sports, literature, arts, or public speaking, clubs help you discover your passions, develop valuable skills, and connect with like-minded students.

🎨 Avinya

A creative arts club that encourages painting, sketching, crafts, digital art, and artistic expression.

🎭 Drusya

The theatre and drama club where students develop acting, storytelling, stage presence, and public speaking skills.

🎵 Saptaswara

A vibrant music club for singers, instrumentalists, and music enthusiasts to perform and collaborate.

Featured Technical Club

💻 Chakravyuha

Technical club focused on programming, competitive coding, hackathons, AI, and software development.

📚 Bookworms

A community for readers and writers who enjoy literature, discussions, and creative expression.
