Campus & Hostel Life

Your hostel will be much more than just a place to stay. It will become your second home where friendships are built, memories are created, and personal growth begins. Explore everything from daily routines and hostel facilities to dining, transportation, campus resources, and student life at Amrita Amaravati.

🏢 Hostel Facilities

🚶 Compact Campus

The campus is compact and walkable. Most places can be reached within 5 minutes, so bicycles are generally not required.

📶 Wi-Fi

Hostels and academic blocks are covered with campus Wi-Fi for academic purposes.

🛏️ Comfortable Rooms

Hostel rooms provide a peaceful environment suitable for studying and academic activities.

📚 Study Friendly

The hostel atmosphere is generally calm and academically focused, making it easier to concentrate on studies.

🍽️ Food & Mess Information

The hostel mess primarily serves vegetarian food.

Meal Approximate Timing
Breakfast 7:30 AM - 8:30 AM
Lunch 1:00 PM - 2:00 PM
Dinner 7:00 PM - 8:30 PM
🥗 Non-vegetarian food is not allowed inside hostel messes or academic blocks. Students who wish to have non-vegetarian food can purchase it from the campus canteen whenever available.

🚌 Transport Facilities

Most students stay in hostels, so the number of day scholars is relatively small.

🚍 Vijayawada Buses

3 dedicated college buses operate between Vijayawada and the campus.

🚍 Guntur Buses

3 dedicated college buses operate between Guntur and the campus.

🏫 Easy Campus Access

Transportation is well organized for day scholars travelling from nearby cities.

🌟 Tips to Settle In During Your First Week

Make Friends Early

Talk to classmates and hostel mates during orientation.

Explore the Campus

Spend time locating important places like classrooms, library and mess.

Attend Every Class

The first few weeks help you understand faculty expectations.

Join Clubs

Participate in technical and cultural clubs to build skills and friendships.

Maintain a Routine

Balancing academics, activities and rest will make college life easier.

Ask Seniors

Seniors can help with academics, clubs and campus-related doubts.
