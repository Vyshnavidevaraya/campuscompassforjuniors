About the Art Club

The Art Club at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is a vibrant community of creative thinkers, passionate about visual storytelling, painting, sketching, and more. Our mission is to inspire students to discover their inner artist and provide a platform for them to showcase their talents. We host regular workshops, art exhibitions, mural projects, and even collaborate with other departments to bring art into every corner of campus. Whether you're a beginner or a professional, the Art Club welcomes you with open arms and a splash of color!
