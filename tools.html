🚀 Freshers First Week Action Plan

Complete these important tasks during your first week to ensure a smooth transition into college life and stay ahead from Day One.


  • ✅ Create and verify your AUMS account.
  • ✅ Install and explore the Student Portal App.
  • ✅ Login to My Amrita Portal and check your details.
  • ✅ Activate and regularly monitor your official student email.
  • ✅ Join class, hostel, and department WhatsApp groups.
  • ✅ Save mentor, faculty, and important administrative contacts.
  • ✅ Bookmark AUMS and My Amrita Portal for quick access.
  • ✅ Create a GitHub account and upload your first repository.
  • ✅ Explore NPTEL, GeeksforGeeks, and other learning resources.
  • ✅ Solve your first coding problem on LeetCode.
🎯 Completing these tasks in your first week will help you stay organized, avoid confusion, and start your academic journey with confidence.

🎯 Senior Advice

Senior Advice: Start building your skills from Day One. A GitHub profile, consistent coding practice on LeetCode, and NPTEL certifications can significantly strengthen your resume over the next four years. Small daily efforts often create the biggest long-term results.
