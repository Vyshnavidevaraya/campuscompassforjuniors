About the Sports Crew

The Sports Crew at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham brings together passionate athletes and sports enthusiasts. Our team organizes regular fitness sessions, inter-college sports events, and various workshops that promote teamwork and physical well-being. We cater to all skill levels and provide opportunities for both competitive and recreational athletes to thrive in their favorite sports.
